      <h2><b> Search Engine Optimization </b></h2>
      <p>Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving your website so that search engines like Google or Bing rank it higher when people search for topics related to your business or interests.</p>

      <h4>What are the three pillars of SEO?</h4>
      <p> <b> Technical optimization:</b> Technical Optimization is the process of completing activities on your site that are designed to improve SEO but are not related to content. It often happens behind the scenes. A simple example of technical optimization is submitting your sitemap to Google.</p>
      <p> <b>On-Page Optimization:</b> On-Page Optimization is the process of ensuring the content on your site is relevant and provides a great user experience. It includes targeting the right keywords within your content and can be done through a content management system like WordPress, Wix, Drupal, Joomla, Magento, or Shopify. </p>
      <p> <b>Off-Page Optimization:</b>  Off-Page Optimization is the process of enhancing your site’s search engine rankings through activities outside of the site. This is largely driven by high-quality backlinks, which help to build the site’s reputation.  </p>
